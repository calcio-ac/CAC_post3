METHODOLOGY
• Sample: players across WC 2026 squads.
• Club attribution: each player is credited to their parent club at squad announcement. Loanees are credited to the parent club (e.g. Marcus Rashford → Manchester United, not Barcelona). This is the same rule FIFA uses on the official roster.
• Reserves merged: B-teams / reserve sides count toward their senior team (e.g. Barcelona B → Barcelona). This is why Barcelona's count includes Hamza Abdelkarim.
• League = club country: a league here means the country its club is based in, not a specific division. England covers Premier League + EFL combined.
• Continent = club continent, not the national team's continent. A Senegalese player at Arsenal counts toward Europe.
• Mapping coverage: % of players are mapped to a club country. The remainder (mostly obscure spelling variants) are tagged "Unmapped" and excluded from league/continent counts but still appear in club totals.
• Source: FBref / FIFA roster + manual cleanup.
• Where the official Barça press release says 16 blaugranes, this dataset shows 14: the difference is Rashford (counted at Man Utd) and Anthony Gordon (counted at Newcastle).
